Curriculum Vitae

Rigorous science serving
organizations

I hold a PhD in Regional Economics from the Universidad Autónoma de Coahuila and I am a member of the National System of Researchers (SNI), Level I. Since 2019 I have led research on organizations at the Institute for Purpose and Integral Wellbeing of Universidad Tecmilenio, and since 2020 I have been a professor at the Business School of Tecnológico de Monterrey.

For six years I have served as an advisor to business executives in the Master's in Positive Leadership at Universidad Tecmilenio, impacting hundreds of employees. I am also a member of the Círculo Azul program at Tec de Monterrey, advising entrepreneurs on how to scale their ventures.

"I was a member of the Specialized Technical Group on Income and Wellbeing Measurement (GTMIB), the team responsible for developing new wellbeing and income indicators for Mexico, in collaboration with INEGI."

I am a founding member of the Latin American Association for Human Flourishing Studies (ALEFH) and a member of the editorial board of the journal Applied Research in Quality of Life. My methodological expertise includes econometrics, psychometrics, and scale development for measuring wellbeing, with an interest in applying Big Data and Machine Learning to economics and psychology.

Scientific Research

Research Areas & Publications

Around 40 published articles and book chapters, with more than 600 citations in the scientific literature. Speaker at conferences of the International Society for Quality-of-Life Studies (ISQOLS) in the United States, South Korea, Austria, the Netherlands, Luxembourg, and Argentina.

Organizational Wellbeing

Measuring and promoting wellbeing at work: the BEAT model, validation of instruments (IWH-BEAT, PERMA-Profiler, Healthy Mind Index), and its relationship with productivity and employee turnover.

Meaningful Work

Life purpose, happiness at work, and turnover intentions: empirical evidence on how a sense of meaning transforms employee performance and retention.

Entrepreneurship

Human flourishing as an enabler of entrepreneurial intention in Latin America, and the job satisfaction and quality of life of entrepreneurs in Mexico.

Consulting

Organizational wellbeing
as a competitive advantage

I work with companies and executive teams on the design, measurement, and improvement of employee wellbeing, using scientifically validated methodologies and experience measuring the wellbeing of more than 100,000 workers in Mexico through the Factor Wellbeing project.

— 01

Wellbeing Diagnosis & Measurement

Applying validated psychometric instruments (BEAT, PERMA, Healthy Mind Index) to diagnose the real state of wellbeing in your organization, with advanced analytics and national benchmarks.

— 02

Wellbeing & Meaningful Work Strategies

Evidence-based interventions to strengthen purpose, trust, and appreciation within teams, reducing turnover and boosting engagement.

— 03

Executive Advisory & Positive Leadership

Six years advising business executives in the Master's in Positive Leadership: developing leaders who create high-performing, human-flourishing environments.

— 04

Keynotes & Executive Training

Talks, workshops, and in-company programs on the economics of happiness, ROI on wellbeing, and more productive organizations.
